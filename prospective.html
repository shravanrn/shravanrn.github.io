(Last Updated: Jan 2025)

PhD program applicants: Our lab is looking for candidates with interest and experience in security, programming languages, or architecture. The application form is available at the UT Austin website. You are also welcome to email me if you have questions.

Current Masters/Undergrad at UT Austin: If you are a current student at UT Austin, I am happy to discuss research projects you can try out or participate in. I generally recommend you do this with a lighter course load, as research projects tend to eat up a lot of time. Please email me to setup a meeting.

Masters/Undergrad program applicants: If you are interested in applying to the UT Austin masters/undergrad program, please note all applications go through a central committee, over which I have no control, so you do not have to email me. You are welcome to list me as faculty you would like to work with in your application/SOP. After you start the program, feel free to send me an email to setup an appointment if you want to chat about research.

Current Masters/Undergrad at other universities: If you are not a student at UT Austin and are interested in working with me, please note our lab does not offer any remote or short-term internships. Instead, I would suggest considering applying to the graduate program at UT Austin.

Postdoc program applicants: At this time, our lab does not have any positions open for Postdocs. This may change in the future, and I will do my best to keep this page updated. If you see that this page's last updated date has not been updated in over 6 months, feel free to email me to inquire if things have changed.
